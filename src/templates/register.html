Welcome to EmissiTrack Pro

User Instructions:

1. In the text field at the top, type the company name, ticker, or ISIN.


2. Choose the matching “Search Type” radio button.


3. Click “Go.”


4. View the left column for the extracted emissions tables.


5. Check the right column for the emissions chart and the chatbot.


6. (Optional) Type a question for the chatbot and click “Ask.”


7. (Optional) If you need to cancel the data retrieval process, click Stop RAG.


That’s it! The page is meant to help you quickly find, visualize, and chat about greenhouse gas emissions data from the company’s sustainability reports.
